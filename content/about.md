I'm Victor Hang — Senior Technical Lead and infrastructure nerd based in Bordeaux, France.

I work on platforms. Kubernetes, NixOS, automation, networking — anything that keeps systems reliable and engineers productive. I've spent years in the SRE and DevOps space, building Kubernetes-as-a-Service platforms, engineering sovereign cloud infrastructure, and writing Golang controllers to automate things that shouldn't be done by hand.

This blog is where I write about the things I build, the tools I use, and the problems I run into. Expect posts on Kubernetes internals, infrastructure patterns, NixOS, and whatever else I'm tinkering with.

Outside of work, I'm into running, lifting, swimming, and going to concerts — classical, jazz, j-pop, funk, electro, you name it. I also travel when I can. Japan and Italy are favourites. When I'm not outside, you'll find me deep in cyberpunk lore — anything CD Projekt Red touches, and the Persona series from Atlus. I'm also big into simracing.
